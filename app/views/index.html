V5 - Clean 2015 Scheme redundancy estimate

This design includes the integration of MIS where member details are pulled from the member's record in Compendia Touch. Updates to the design were made based on research findings from UR rounds 11 & 12.

User journeys for testing

Journey 1 (Scenario 1)

  • Member details retrieved from MIS
  • Record on Compendia Touch is updated for the previous financial year
  • Redundancy date is in the current financial year
  • Redundancy payment does not meet the cap costs letter
  • Case requires quality checking
  • Membership number to enter - 11697385

Journey 2 (Scenario 2)

  • Interest of efficiency case
  • Member details are not retrieved from MIS
  • Record on Compendia Touch is updated for the previous financial year
  • Redundancy date is in the next financial year
  • Case does not need quality checking
  • Membership number to enter - 67092299

Journey 3 (Scenario 3)

  • Estimate request received by AW295 form
  • Record on Compendia is not updated for the previous financial year
  • Member has multiple employments
  • Redundancy date is in the next financial year
  • Case requires quality checking
  • Membership number to enter - 78843423

Journey stopper (Scenario 4)

  • Record on Compendia is updated for the previous financial year
  • Estimate request received by Bulk spreadsheet
  • Member has served less than 2 years service
  • Date of estimate to enter - 20/05/2026
  • Membership number to enter - 67233221

Journey stopper (Scenario 5)

  • Record on Compendia is updated for the previous financial year
  • Estimate request received by Bulk spreadsheet
  • Member is over 67 years at date of estimate
  • Date of estimate to enter - 15/06/2026
  • Membership number to enter - 67233232

Journey stopper (Scenario 6)

  • Estimate request received by AW295 form
  • Member has an ERRBO
