Get a redundancy estimate

Use this service to calculate redundancy estimates for NHS Pension Scheme members.

Before you start

We can only calculate redundancy estimates for members who meet certain criteria by their redundancy date. They must:

  • have at least 2 years of qualifying membership within the Scheme
  • have 2 years of continuous service verified by the employer
  • have reached the minimum pension age of the Scheme they are part of
  • not have a GP practice as their Employing Authority
  • have redundancy provisions in place, if part of a direction body

Completing the calculation

When you start the calculation, we'll ask you to enter all the member data we need to get an estimate. After the data is submitted, the service will create 2 documents:

  • A calculation sheet
  • An estimate letter, which you'll need to send to the employer
