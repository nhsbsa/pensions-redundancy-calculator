Make sure the member is not affected by one or more of these circumstances

You can only use this service to calculate estimates for members who:

  • do not have pensions on divorce debit
  • do not have additional pensions (APs)
  • do not have an early retirement reduction buy-out (ERRBO)
  • do not have transfers
  • are not over the normal pension age (NPA)
  • are not a McCloud comparison calculation

If the member is affected

You cannot complete the calculation using this service. The record will need to be allocated to the correct pension admin for this type of case.
