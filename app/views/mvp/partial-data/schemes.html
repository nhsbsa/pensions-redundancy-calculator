Which scheme is the member eligible for redundancy on?

Age related benefits are dependant on whether the member has reached the state/normal pension age within their schemes. If the member has reached the normal pension age in one of their schemes, they are not eligible to claim redundancy benefits of that scheme.

To find out the member's age you can find this information in the Service history set in Compendia.

The member must be under the age of 60 to be eligible for redundancy benefits on both schemes.
The member must be under the age of 65 to be eligible for redundancy benefits on both schemes.
