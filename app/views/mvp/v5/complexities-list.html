Check the member's circumstances

You cannot continue with the calculation if any of these circumstances apply to the member.

The member must not have:

  • pensions on divorce debit
  • additional pensions
  • early retirement reduction buy-out (ERRBO)
  • transfers
  • breaks in service over 5 years (split benefits)
  • an earnings cap
  • a redundancy date in the past
  • McCloud comparison eligibility

The member must not be:

  • over the normal pension age (opens in a new tab) for the 2015 Scheme
  • employed by a Welsh employer
  • partially retired
  • a practitioner
  • a re-employed pensioner
  • employed outside of Agenda for Change terms and conditions
