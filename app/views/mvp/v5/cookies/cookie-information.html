What are cookies?

Cookies are files saved on your phone, tablet or computer when you visit a website.

They store information about how you use the website, such as the pages you visit.

We use 2 types of cookie. You can choose which cookies you are happy for us to use.

How we use cookies

We use these essential cookies to make our website work. They include:

  • make our website work, for example by keeping it secure
  • remember your progress through a form (for example a licence application)
  • allow you to return to a previous page in your journey through the service
  • store your cookie consent choices
