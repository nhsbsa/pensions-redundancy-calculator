Does the case need to be checked?

The consolidation phase requires you to complete 10 cases in a row correctly. After 10 cases have been completed correctly, you are no longer in the consolidation phase.

You can check to see if the case needs to go to quality checking by booking out the case in Operations Manager (OM).

If the case needs quality checking or is part of the consolidation phase

If the case needs to be reviewed by the quality checking team or is part of the consolidation phase then the calculation sheet and estimate letter will be downloaded onto your device. You will then need to share the documents.

If the case does not need checked

If the case does not need to go to quality checking or the consolidation phase then the files will be uploaded to the member's record in EDM.
